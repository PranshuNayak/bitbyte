BITBYTE- THE PROGRAMMING CLUB

ABOUT

Bitbyte is the club of developers, designers and prorammers of our institute IIITDMJ. Here at bitbyte we encourage students to learn new technologies. We held regular sessions to provide guidance to the students about new endeavours in Science and Technology.

  • MACHINE LEARNING
  • DEEP LEARNING

  • ETHICAL HACKING
  • COMPETITIVE PROGRAMMING

Frequently Asked Questions

The registration fee is decided by the institute and it varies annualy. However is it not very high, students can pay it.
